We’re a group of people who came together with the goal of creating a startup scene in Portugal that’s more connected, inclusive and wise.

In our Slack community you can find and connect with fellow founders and ecosystem partners who contribute wisdom and support, as well as many channels of curated content: events, news, advice, job listings and startup opportunities updated every day by people who work on them.

If you want to keep up to date with the ever-growing startup scene in Portugal, 351 might be a good first door for you. If you are new to Portugal we have many connections and resources to help you settle in. Our arms are open and every member is reachable without a middle person.

If you’re someone who believes in building meaningful long-term relationships instead of handing out business cards, 351 is for you.
