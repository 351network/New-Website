We’re a group of people who came together with the goal of creating a startup scene in Portugal that’s more connected, inclusive and wise.

In our Slack Community you can find and connect with fellow founders and ecosystem partners who contribute wisdom and support, as well as many channels of curated content: events, news, advice, job listings and startup opportunities updated every day by people who work on them.

If you want to keep up to date with the ever-growing startup scene or get settled in Portugal, 351 might be a good first door for you! Our arms are open and every member is reachable without a middle person.

To join our community you have to be invited because we want to make sure the 351 community is a good fit for you and remains a safe space for everyone. If you’re someone who believes in building long-term relationships instead of handing out business cards, the 351 community is for you.
